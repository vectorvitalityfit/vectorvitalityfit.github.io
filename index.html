THE 3 LAWS OF MUSCULUS CALCULUS

1. DERIVATIVE = GAIN ACCELERATOR (dM/dt)

"Your muscles grow when you hack the RATE of change."

  • Stall Point: When dM/dt ≈ 0 (plateau = growth flatlines)
  • Hack It:
    • Increase Stimulus (k): Swap 1 exercise/week
    • Crush Recovery Debt (c): Sleep 7+ hrs
    • Track Progress: Log lifts & watch slope

2. INTEGRAL = TOTAL GAINZ (∫ Volume dt)

"Muscle is the AREA under your training curve—not just max weight."

  • Bro Math: 1 Rep Max(1RM) = height of curve
  • Calculus Math: Total volume = width * height
  • Hack It:
    • 5% Volume Rule: Add 5% total reps/weight weekly
    • Time Under Tension = ∫ Force dt: Slow eccentrics (3 sec down)

3. OPTIMAL BAR PATH = MIN/MAX PROBLEM

"Extraordinary lifts are just calculus in disguise."

  • Bench Press Hack:
    • Standard Path: Straight up/down → energy leaks
    • Calculus Path: Smooth curve (slight backward arc)
  • How to Test: Film lifts & compare to elite bar paths

THE FULL MUSCLE EQUATION

dM/dt = k·(Mmax – M) – c

  • M = Current muscle mass
  • k = Training stimulus (0.3 → 0.7 for advanced lifters)
  • c = Recovery debt (sleep, stress, diet)
